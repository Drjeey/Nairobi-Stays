GA4 Custom Events Being Sent

page_view
Fires automatically on every visit. Tracked by GA4 natively — no extra code needed.
book_now_click
Fires when a visitor taps "Book Now". Includes listing_title, listing_category, listing_price, and KES value.
select_item
GA4 standard ecommerce event. Populates the Items dimension in GA reports for funnel analysis.
filter_used
Fires when a neighbourhood or budget filter is clicked. Includes filter_name param so you see which area is most popular.
whatsapp_cta_click
Fires on WhatsApp buttons in the nav and hero. The cta_source param tells you which button drove the click.
